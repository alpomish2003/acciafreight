About Us

Accia Freight LLC is a trusted interstate trucking company based in Cincinnati, OH with additional operations in Winston-Salem, NC. Established in 2012, we specialize in full truckload shipping across the 48 contiguous states. Our fleet of modern Volvo and Freightliner trucks is maintained to the highest standards for safety and efficiency.

Our Services

  • General Freight
  • Building Materials
  • Grain, Feed, and Hay
  • U.S. Mail
  • Chemicals
  • Construction Materials
